<p><i>FlutCrack</i> lets you crack hashes by comparing them against a dictionary of words.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Hash cracking and algorithms identifying</li><li>Support for multiple hash algorithms: MD5, SHA-1, SHA-224, SHA-256, SHA-384, SHA-512, SHA-512/224, SHA-512/256</li><li>User-friendly interface with text input and dropdown selection for hash algorithms</li><li>Dynamic wordlists support ( from web, device memory or in-app created )</li><li>Words hasher</li></ul><p><b>Usage:</b></p><ol><li>Open the FlutCrack application on your device or emulator.</li><li>Before cracking your hash, go to the wordlists section, create and add words to your wordlist. This step is required only on the first run, as the previous wordlist will be saved for future use.</li><li>Go back to the home screen, pick the wordlist you want to use from those you created, from your device memory or from the web.</li><li>Enter the hash you want to crack in the text field.</li><li>Select the hash algorithm from the dropdown menu (if you know it).</li><li>Press the action button to start the cracking process.</li><li>The result will be displayed below the input fields.</li></ol><p><b>Caution:</b> The FlutCrack team does not assume any responsibility for the improper use of the app.</p>
